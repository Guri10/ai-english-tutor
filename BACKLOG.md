# Backlog

Suggestions, improvements, and design changes that aren't yet issues.
This is a holding pen — jot things down here as they come up, then convert
whichever ones are ready into GitHub issues (`docs/agents/issue-tracker.md`
has the conventions) when there's time to scope them properly.

Not a design doc and not a decision log — `current_state.md` and
`docs/superpowers/specs/` are the source of truth for what's actually been
decided and built. This is just unsorted raw material.

## Format

One entry per idea. Keep it short — a sentence or two of what and why is
enough; the real scoping happens when it becomes an issue.

```
- **Short title** — what/why. (optional: source/context)
```
